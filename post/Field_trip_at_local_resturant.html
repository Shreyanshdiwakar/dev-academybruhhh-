A Delicious Learning Experience: Dev Academy's Field Trip to Domino's Pizza.

At Dev Academy, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom walls. Recently, our students had the exciting opportunity to embark on a field trip to Domino's Pizza, where they combined fun with education in a unique and engaging way.

The Adventure Begins

On a bright and cheerful day, our students, dressed in their colorful uniforms, eagerly boarded the bus to Domino's Pizza. The excitement was palpable as they chatted about the delicious pizzas they were about to make and taste. The trip was part of our initiative to provide hands-on learning experiences that foster curiosity and practical knowledge.

Behind the Scenes at Domino's

Upon arrival, the students were warmly welcomed by the friendly Domino's staff, who were just as excited to host the young visitors. The staff took the students on an informative tour of the facility, explaining the various processes involved in making their favorite pizzas. From the preparation of the dough to the careful selection of fresh ingredients, the students got a behind-the-scenes look at what goes into creating the perfect pizza./p>

The tour also included a demonstration of how pizzas are assembled and baked. The students watched in awe as the pizzas went into the oven and emerged hot and ready to eat. This hands-on learning experience was both educational and inspiring, giving the students a new appreciation for the art of pizza making.

Hands-On Pizza Making .

The highlight of the trip was undoubtedly the pizza-making session. The students donned aprons and chef hats, ready to try their hand at making their own pizzas. Under the guidance of the expert Domino's staff, they rolled out their dough, spread the sauce, and added their favorite toppings. This interactive activity allowed the students to express their creativity while learning about the importance of teamwork and precision.
