Dive deep into the ancient culture of Jhansi

March 11, 2024

Exploring History: Dev Academy's Visit to Jhansi Fort.

At Dev Academy, we believe in bringing history to life for our students. Our recent field trip to Jhansi Fort provided an immersive educational experience that allowed our students to step back in time and explore the rich cultural heritage of India.

Stepping into the Past

On a crisp November morning, our students, dressed in their school uniforms, set out on an exciting journey to Jhansi Fort. The fort, a symbol of bravery and resilience, stands as a testament to India's rich historical legacy. As the students approached the majestic gates, the sense of stepping into a different era was palpable.

A Guided Tour of Jhansi Fort

Upon arrival, the students were greeted by a knowledgeable guide who led them through the sprawling grounds of the fort. The guide recounted tales of valiant warriors and historic battles, painting vivid pictures of the past. The students listened intently, eager to learn about the fort's significance and the heroic Rani Lakshmibai, the Queen of Jhansi, who played a crucial role in the Indian Rebellion of 1857.

The tour included visits to key landmarks within the fort, such as the Rani Mahal, the place where Rani Lakshmibai lived, and the Baradari, a beautiful pavilion offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. Each location within the fort sparked curiosity and admiration among the students, allowing them to connect with history in a tangible way.

Educational Insights and Activities.

The visit to Jhansi Fort was not just about sightseeing; it was an educational journey. The students participated in various activities designed to enhance their understanding of history. They engaged in discussions about the significance of the fort and its role in India's fight for independence. These activities encouraged critical thinking and allowed the students to express their reflections on what they had learned..
